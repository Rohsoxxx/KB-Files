Install Microsoft Outlook on iPhone

Free to download. Takes about 2 minutes to set up.

iPhone with iOS 16 or later
Apple ID
Internet connection
1

Open the App Store

Find the blue App Store icon on your iPhone home screen or in your App Library and tap it.

Look for App Store — it has a blue background with a white "A" made of sticks.

2

Search for Outlook

Tap the Search tab at the bottom of the screen, then type Microsoft Outlook in the search bar at the top.

Type "Microsoft Outlook" and tap Search on your keyboard to find the official app.

3

Tap "Get" to download

Find Microsoft Outlook in the results — it has a blue icon with a white envelope. Tap the blue Get button next to it.

You may be asked to confirm with Face ID, Touch ID, or your Apple ID password.

4

Open Outlook

Once the download finishes, tap Open in the App Store, or find the Outlook icon on your home screen.

Outlook opens to a setup screen. It's free — no purchase needed.

5

Add your email account

Tap Add Account, enter your email address (Microsoft 365, Outlook.com, Gmail, Yahoo, and others are all supported), then enter your password and follow the prompts.

Sign in with your Microsoft, Google, or other email credentials when prompted.

6

Allow notifications (optional)

Outlook will ask if you want to receive email notifications. Tap Allow to get alerts for new messages, or Don't Allow to skip — you can change this later in Settings.

Tap Allow to get notified when new emails arrive. You can adjust this any time.

All set! Your email, calendar, and contacts will sync automatically. You can add more accounts anytime by going to Settings → Add Mail Account inside Outlook.
